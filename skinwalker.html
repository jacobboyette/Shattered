The forest seems to shift around you, the familiar path dissolving into a maze of twisted roots and dense shadows. The air is unnaturally still, yet every rustle and crack of a branch feels amplified, as though the trees themselves are whispering warnings. Ahead, a figure steps into view. At first glance, it seems human—a traveler, perhaps—but something is off. Its movements are too fluid, too unnatural, like a predator mimicking prey. The figure tilts its head, and for a fleeting moment, its face flickers, distorting into something grotesque before snapping back to a hollow, unnatural smile. A chill runs down your spine as the stories come rushing back. A creature that wears the skins of others, luring the unsuspecting deeper into the woods. The figure takes a step closer, its voice a perfect mimicry of someone familiar. 'Lost, are we?' it says, though the words feel like a trap. The forest seems to close in, and you realize—you’re not alone, and whatever is with you is anything but human.
